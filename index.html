Why Does Password Strength Matter?

Re-using the same password across many platforms—like Gmail, Ecocash, WhatsApp, Facebook, or your bank's app—may seem easier, but it puts you at serious risk. If someone gets hold of one password, they could potentially access everything, from your money to your personal messages.

To protect yourself, always use a strong, unique password for every account. This makes it harder for cybercriminals to hack you. Use the Password Strength Test Chart to check how secure your passwords are.

What Is a Password Strength Tester?

A password strength tester is a tool that checks how difficult it would be for someone—or a computer program—to guess your password. It looks at things like password length, how random it is, and if it includes numbers, symbols, or capital letters.

It's a quick and easy way to check if your password can stand up to hackers—especially for key accounts like your mobile banking, email, or company systems.

How Does It Work?

These testers calculate how long it would take a hacker using automated tools to guess your password by trying every combination of characters. The stronger the password, the longer it takes to crack—sometimes even hundreds of years.
